      Bedford-Stuyvesant Bleeds: City Politics, Police Inaction, More Dead Pedestrians

      Written June 17, 2025 (Revised June 17, 2025 – Fixed broken link, cleaned citations)

      Precinct 79: Jan 1, 2022 - Jun 17, 2025

      The Toll on Bedford-Stuyvesant Streets

      A man steps off the curb. An SUV keeps going. The man does not get up. In the last twelve months, Precinct 79 saw 1 death and 5 serious injuries. There were 382 injuries in total. The dead are not numbers. They are neighbors, parents, children. The street does not care who you are.

      Patterns of Harm: Who Pays the Price

      SUVs and cars did most of the killing. In three years, they caused 4 deaths and 5 serious injuries to pedestrians. Trucks and buses killed one more. Bikes injured seven. The old and the young are not spared. In the last year, 29 children were hurt. One man, age 45-54, died. The street is a lottery. The odds are bad.

      Leadership: Action and Evasion

      When the city removed the protected bike lane on Bedford Avenue, it was not for lack of danger. The area had “the second largest numbers of pedestrian fatalities and crashes in the city” before the lane went in, said DOT Commissioner Ydanis Rodriguez. But after complaints, the city ripped it out. Council Member Lincoln Restler called it “just pure politics. That is not how safety decisions on our streets should be made” (CBS New York). The city listened to those who wanted the lane gone. The dead and injured cannot speak.

      The Role of the Police: Tools Unused

      Precinct 79 has the power to enforce the law. They can ticket speeders, reckless drivers, and those who fail to yield. They can target crash hotspots. They can make the street less deadly. They just need to act.

      What You Can Do

      Demand action. Call your council member. Call the mayor. Call the precinct. Tell them the numbers are not enough. Tell them the dead deserve more than silence. Tell them to enforce the law, protect the vulnerable, and fix the street. Do not wait for another name on the list. Take action now.
