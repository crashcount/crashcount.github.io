      Who’s Injuring and Killing Pedestrians in District 8?

      Ayala’s District Bleeds: Lower the Speed, Stop the Next Siren

      Written July 10, 2025 (Revised July 10, 2025 – Minor stats refresh)

      District 8: Jan 1, 2022 - Jul 10, 2025

      Blood on the Crosswalks

      A Ford Mustang turned left at East 149th and Courtlandt, jumped the curb, and plowed into six people. The driver ran. The pain stayed. “People were yelling, were in pain, so yelling, crying, it was very upsetting,” said Vivian Cole. All six went to the hospital. They were lucky. This time, no one died.

      In the last twelve months, District 8 saw 7 deaths and 17 serious injuries from crashes. 1,049 people were hurt. The dead included a 22-year-old passenger on a moped, a 53-year-old man crushed by an SUV, and a child killed crossing the Major Deegan. The numbers do not bleed, but the streets do.

      The Shape of Danger

      Cars and SUVs did the most harm. They killed five, left hundreds injured, and broke bodies at the curb. Trucks and buses killed two. Motorcycles and mopeds took two more. Bikes killed one. The city counts the bodies. The city moves on.

      Council Member Ayala: Action and Silence

      Council Member Diana Ayala has backed bills to ban parking near crosswalks, light up step streets, and expand greenways. She voted to legalize jaywalking, ending a law that punished the desperate and the slow. She co-sponsored measures to target fake plates and curb dangerous trucks. But the carnage continues. “We thought it was a bomb or something, because we are all panicking around here,” said Christina Sieh.

      The council can do more. The city can lower the speed limit to 20 mph. It can redesign every deadly street. It can end the reign of repeat offenders. Every day of delay is another family broken.

      Call to Action

      Call Council Member Ayala. Call the Mayor. Demand a 20 mph speed limit and real protection for people on foot and bike. The blood on the street is not an accident. It is a choice. Change it.
